About Saber GSE

Saber Fame Studios and Entertainment was founded in 2018 by GitHub user AngstyPotato.
With an interest in video games and their design processes stemming all the way back to 2006, AngstyPotato has now begun the journey as an indie developer and producer.
Their most recent project, You Do Voudou, is currently in the works, and AngstyPotato hopes that it will not only be a fun and adventurous experience,
but an inspiration to others who wish to use their creativity and coding skills to develop their own works of interactive art.
